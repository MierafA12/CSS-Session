Welcome to My Simple Website

The lion (Panthera leo) is a majestic big cat and one of the most recognizable animals in the world. Known as the "king of the jungle," lions are native to sub-Saharan Africa, though small populations can also be found in the Gir Forest of India. Lions are social animals, living in groups known as prides, which typically consist of related females, their cubs, and a few males.
